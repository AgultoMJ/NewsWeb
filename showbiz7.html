5 stylish small designer bags worn by Gabbi Garcia


Gabbi Garcia with her mini-bags (L-R): Fendi Nano Fendigraphy bag, Loewe Amazona 16 top handle, and Louis Vuitton Over The Moon bag

Headline

The noughties fashion is currently in vogue in the fashion world, and with it, the popularity of designer mini bags.

Although these small luxury bags may seem quite impractical, dozens of celebrities have hopped on the designer mini-bag bandwagon.

GMA-7's actress/fashionista Gabbi Garcia is clearly one of them, as the designer mini bag has become an essential accessory in her fashion arsenal.

Here are some stylish and petite designer purses we've spotted on her OOTD posts on Instagram.

FENDI In March 2023, Gabbi jet-setted to Taiwan and brought along a gorgeous green Nano Fendigraphy bag.

The Mga Lihim Ni Urduja actress expertly incorporated the vibrant accessory into her chic, monochromatic blue Fendi ensemble.

In case you're wondering how much this itty bitty designer bag is, it's available for SGD1,750 or PHP73,588.51 on the brand's official website.
